Here is a basic outline for a simple e-commerce website:
Home Page
Header with logo, navigation menu, and shopping cart icon
Hero section with featured products or promotions
Product showcase with grid layout
Call-to-action (CTA) buttons for shopping or learning more


Product Page
Product image gallery
Product description and details
Price and availability information
Add to cart button
Related products section


Shopping Cart
List of products added to cart
Quantity and total price updates
Remove product button
Checkout button


Checkout
Login or guest checkout options
Shipping and billing information forms
Payment method selection (e.g. credit card, PayPal)
Order summary and total price
Place order button


Product Categories
Category pages with product listings
Filtering and sorting options


Search
Search bar with autocomplete suggestions
Search results page with product listings


User Account
Login and registration forms
Order history and tracking information
Account settings and profile management
This is a basic outline, and you can add or remove sections as needed. You'll also need to consider the design, user experience, and functionality of each page.
